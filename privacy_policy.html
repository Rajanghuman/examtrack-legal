📋 1. Introduction

ExamTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application developed and operated by Rajan Ghuman, an individual developer based in Amritsar, Punjab, India.

ExamTrack helps users in North India track government job notifications, exam dates, results, admit cards, and study material.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and what rights you have over your data. By using ExamTrack, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Legal Compliance: This Privacy Policy is compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and the DPDP Rules, 2025. As a Data Fiduciary under the Act, we process your personal data only for lawful purposes with your consent. You have the right to access, correct, and erase your personal data as described in Section 7 of this policy. This policy also complies with Google Play Store requirements and the Information Technology Act, 2000.
📊 2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use the app, we collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • State / Location (e.g., Punjab, Haryana, UP)
  • Educational Qualification
  • Category (General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS)
  • Job category preferences (SSC, Railway, Banking, etc.)

2.2 Information Generated by Your Activity

As you use the app, we store:

  • Jobs you have saved
  • Jobs you have tracked (with application status)
  • Jobs you have marked as applied
  • Results and marks you have entered
  • Notification preferences you set

2.3 Information Collected Automatically

Firebase (operated by Google) may automatically collect crash reports and error logs to help us fix bugs. We do not run our own analytics beyond this.

We do NOT collect: Your location, camera/microphone data, contacts, call logs, device advertising ID, or browsing history outside the app.

🔒 4. Data Storage and Security
  • All user data is stored on Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore) on Google's secure, encrypted cloud infrastructure.
  • We implement Firebase security rules so only you can access your own data.
  • Your password is never stored by us — Firebase Authentication handles it with industry-standard encryption.
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
Data Retention: Your data is kept as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You may request account deletion by emailing us at rajanghuman13@gmail.com.

⚖️ 7. Your Rights Under India's DPDP Act 2023

As a user in India, you have the following rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Update or correct your data through Edit Profile or by contacting us
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data within 30 days
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: File a complaint with us or with India's Data Protection Board
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate another person to exercise your rights on your behalf

👤 8. Age Requirements

ExamTrack is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older (government job applicants).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will delete their account and associated data promptly.

⚠️ If you are under 18, please do not use this app. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at rajanghuman13@gmail.com.
🔔 9. Push Notifications

ExamTrack may send you push notifications about:

  • New government job vacancies

You can opt-in or opt-out of notifications at any time through your phone's notification settings or within the ExamTrack app settings. We do not send promotional messages from third parties.
